Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how Malbast collects, uses, and protects your personal data.

1. Introduction

At Malbast, we respect your privacy. This policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

By using our website or services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Data

  • Name and contact information (email, phone number, address)
  • Payment and billing details
  • Preferences or messages you send us directly

Non-Personal Data

  • Usage data: Pages visited, session duration, click behavior
  • Device data: Browser, operating system, IP address
  • Cookies: Technical data that helps improve our website

3. How We Collect Data

  • Directly: Through forms, orders, or contact requests
  • Automatically: Via cookies and analytics tools
  • From third parties: Through partners or social media

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To process and deliver orders
  • To provide customer support
  • To send updates and offers
  • To improve our services through analytics and feedback
  • To comply with legal obligations

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share it only when necessary for:

  • Deliveries (couriers, payment processors)
  • Legal compliance or court orders
  • Protection of our rights and customers

6. Data Security

We use modern technologies to keep your data safe, including:

  • SSL encrypted connections
  • Password-protected systems and access control
  • Regular security audits

7. Cookies

Our site uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can disable cookies in your browser, but some site functions may be limited.

8. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Request data portability

To exercise your rights, please contact us via the details below.
